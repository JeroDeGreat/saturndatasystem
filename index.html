Technology

The infrastructure is a trust signal. Treat it like one.

Saturn has unusually strong technical proof for an early-stage telecom brand. This section keeps every important fact from the sample, but gives it a more expensive rhythm.

01

Starlink low-earth-orbit backhaul

Saturn starts with a Starlink dish at the base station, using 25 to 250 Mbps low-latency satellite connectivity instead of waiting for old infrastructure.

02

MikroTik RB5009 routing

The router manages credentials, device limits, speed profiles, firewall rules, and 24/7 visibility across the network.

03

PoE switching for field deployment

Power-over-Ethernet keeps rooftop and pole infrastructure cleaner, more reliable, and easier to maintain.

04

TP-Link EAP650 outdoor access points

Wi-Fi 6, IP67 weather protection, up to 200 metres of radius, and support for 50 or more households on a single access point.

05

Simple customer connection

Customers log in with their account details, keep their monthly service active, and use the internet without thinking about data balance anxiety.
